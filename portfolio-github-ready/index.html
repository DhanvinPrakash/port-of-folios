What makes "me"?

"I love building things. There is something satisfying about starting with random and simple components such as electronics, code, an idea I think of when I'm in the middle of something and all of that into something that actually works. What pulls me in is problem solving, where I need to figure out quality under time constraints, making something stable and beneficial at the same time. I treat engineering like a puzzle, where creativity comes first, rigour second.

Integrated Design Challenge · Graded Project

Rescue Robot · 4WD with Extending PID Arm

One of the only 4WD entries in the cohort, most other teams built 2WD chassis and skipped an extending arm entirely. This arm physically telescopes outward on two 13 kg·cm servo motors, with a PID loop keeping the extension precise and stable under load, giving the robot reach no other team's build had.
