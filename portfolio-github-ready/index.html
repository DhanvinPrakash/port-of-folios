★ Featured — KKH Hospital Collaboration

Neonatal Resuscitation — Guiding Hands, Saving Tiny Lives

Built in direct collaboration with KK Women's and Children's Hospital (KKH), this iOS app is an evidence-based clinical decision support tool for neonatal resuscitation following the SNRC 2021 Guidelines. It calculates weight-based drug dosages, equipment sizes, and APGAR scores in real time — designed to be used at the bedside during critical care.

This app is set to be implemented in KKH and hospitals across Singapore upon project completion.

SwiftUI iOS Clinical Decision Support SNRC 2021 Guidelines Neonatology
IDP Capstone — In Progress KKH Partnership
Integrated Design Challenge

Rescue Rover — 4WD + Extending Claw

For the Integrated Design Challenge, many teams went with a simple claw mounted on a two-wheel-drive base. My group designed for tougher rescue conditions instead: a 4WD drivetrain for grip and stability, paired with an extending claw that can reach into areas where the robot body cannot safely pass.

The goal was a more realistic rescue workflow: cross rough terrain when possible, then extend beyond the chassis to retrieve or assist when debris, gaps, or unstable ground block the robot itself.
